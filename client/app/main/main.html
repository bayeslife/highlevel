What is a software architecture?

Each Business has desired Outcomes.
An Architecture begins with a unique technology Choice from each of a set of Mutually Exclusive Choices.
The Choices can be related to Capabilities that will result from the Choice.
The relationships are debatable and part of the Architecture.
The Capabalities which result will also conditionally relate with various Outcomes.
Measurements can be taken which provide supporting evidence for particular Outcomes and Capabilities.

To develop a software architecture

Identity your business Outcomes.
Identify the Choices.
Identity your business Capabilities
Identity relationships between Choices and Capabilities and Outcomes.
Specify the conditional probability for each relationship.
Review the Outcomes to see the likelihood of the Outcomes.
